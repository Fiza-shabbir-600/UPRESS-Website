Introduction

UPRESS (University of Poonch Rawalakot Engineering Students' society) is a studedicated to empowering engineering students through events, representation, and development opportunities.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading student society promoting innovation, unity, and leadership.

Mission: To organize academic, technical, cultural, and religious activities to uplift student life and growth.

Objectives & Goals

✔ Conduct workshops, seminars, and career talks.
✔ Provide leadership and teamwork opportunities.
✔ Support students' creative and cultural talents.
✔ Strengthen collaboration among departments.

Society Structure

The society is structured with an executive committee, wing heads, and supporting members. Major wings include Academic, Islamic, Cultural, and Sports, each led by dedicated individuals working for student empowerment.
